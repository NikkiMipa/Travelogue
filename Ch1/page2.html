2

May 10, 467

To think a case of tax fraud would take me all the way out here...

I mean, lord forgive we actually investigate when people have been going missing for months (years?), but the second someone catches a few missing deets on a ledger we suddenly gotta send Royale PI on the loose. I get that Mr. Waxing is all like "The New Dawn will rise again if we don't stop them!" but... Old man, I love you. You made me the person I am today, and I'm thankful for that but... It's been over a decade now, maybe the boogeymen got bored of waiting for the morning and went to sleep.

Either way, I'll be in Little Gourd early tomorrow. Then I'll finally be able to put all this nonsense to rest. I've been told all the former New Dawners are magic users, and since there's no way someone could get away with all these crimes for this long without magic, I'll just find the guy casting spells and lock him up. Easy peasy. I mean, I hope they weren't lying about this shield protecting against magic, or that would end pretty badly.

On a brighter note, it's so pretty out here. I only heard stories from SP's travels down here, but now I get to wake up every morning to these misty hills. It really adds to the mystical ambiance everyone talks about with this place. Just a little further down the river and over the ridge and I'm walking the same paths as those sing-songy magicians that ride the Moving Mountains. Wild.
