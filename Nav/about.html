Fantasy Tomorrow: Travelogue

Hey there!

I've been working on this sort of mixed-media story for a while now, and I'm really excited to finally have something to show for it!

Travelogue is a story about a young man trying to be the best person possible in a world full of beautiful weirdos and wacky mysteries. With his companions half-a-tree and an old guy, will he be able to stop the possibly misunderstood baddies standing between our heroes and a brighter tomorrow??

About myself: I'm a 23 year old "linguist" born and raised in Columbus, OH. My family came over from Poland 30ish years ago so now I'm bilingual and kinda good at geography. Sometimes I rollerskate and I love One Piece and Jojo's Bizarre Adventure. I am an appreciator of everything, and I hope I can impart that same feeling of wonder about the world we live in on others!

-Nikki M
