Travelogue

We are compiling the storied accounts of a certain traveller.

Just as the moon pulls oceans along their tidal flows,
The course of history is directed by those who master its repititions.

We are the Order of the Moon.

We aim to provide a point of view through which we can expose humanity's hitherto unrecorded history.
Before we continue I would like to know,

Have you been here before?
